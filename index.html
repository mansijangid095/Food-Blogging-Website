1. Pizza
1. In a mixing bowl - take white flour(maida),add yeast and salt to it.
2. Mix well,Set aside. In another mixing bowl, take luke warm water.Add milk,oil and sugar.Mix well until sugar dissolves.Now add this mixture to flour.
3. Bring together.Knead for atleast 5mins to a smooth dough.Cling wrap and set aside for 1 hour atleast or until the dough doubles.When u check after an hr, the dough would have been raised well.
4. Brush the pizza baking tray with oil,sprinkle flour and keep it ready.Now punch the dough down incase for any air bubbles and then keep aside in a warm place. Roll the dough a little thick Full Recipe

3. Chowmein
1. Chop garlic finely, chop spring onion both white and green potion, slice capsicum/ bell pepper thin/ julienne, shred / thinly slice cabbage and set aside.
2.Heat water in a pot. When it is rolling boil add the noodles and cook till done. When the noodles look cooked drain off the excess water using a strainer and run the cooked noodles in cold water and keep it aside. this step eliminates the noodles sticking to each other.
3.Heat oil in a pan add chopped garlic, saute until aromatic, then add all the veggies. Saute well in high flame for 1 minute. We want the veggies to be crunchy so do not cook for long time. Full Recipe
